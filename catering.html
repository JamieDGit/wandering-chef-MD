Our Catering Services

Every Occasion, Every Detail

Crafted for Life's
Greatest Moments

Whether you're celebrating a sacred milestone or a grand social event, Wandering Chef delivers an exceptional dining experience tailored precisely to your vision, your guests, and your traditions.

Bar & Bat Mitzvahs

Spectacular multi-course banquets, passed hors d'oeuvres, and customized interactive food stations that reflect the joy and grandeur of this coming-of-age milestone.

Vorts

Warm, elegant, and celebratory engagement spreads featuring artisan appetizers, main courses, and custom sweet tables to toast the new couple.

Bris Catering

Traditional morning and afternoon spreads featuring hand-sliced smoked salmon, freshly baked bagels, hot quiches, kugels, and fresh fruit towers.

Barbecues

Casual yet premium outdoor grill events featuring house-marinated meats, gourmet burgers, grilled chicken, fresh salads, and classic summer sides.

Corporate Events

Impressive catering for business lunches, board meetings, corporate parties, and grand gala events that reflect the highest level of professional quality.

Kiddush

Beautiful Shabbat morning synagogue spreads featuring beautifully arranged smoked fish tables, potato and noodle kugels, and assorted pastries.

Food Service / Drop Off

Freshly prepared, fully cooked, and oven-ready meals packaged in disposable trays and delivered directly to your home, office, or venue for effortless hosting.
