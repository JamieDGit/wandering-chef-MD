Meet the Chef

John Caplan,
The Wandering Chef

"Great food isn't just nourishment — it's love, tradition, and memory on a plate."

For over two decades, Chef John Caplan has been the trusted name behind some of the New York area's most beautiful Jewish celebrations. From intimate Bris ceremonies to grand Bar Mitzvah galas, John brings an unmatched passion for elevated kosher cuisine to every event he touches.

As the Wandering Chef, John has traveled, tasted, and trained across the world — bringing those flavors home to your table.
