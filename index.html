Meet the Chef

Jon Kaplan,
The Wandering Chef

"Great food isn't just nourishment — it's love, tradition, and memory on a plate."

For over two decades, Chef Jon Kaplan has been the trusted name behind some of the Maryland area's most beautiful Jewish celebrations. From intimate Bris kiddushes to grand Bar Mitzvah catering, Jon brings an unmatched passion for elevated kosher cuisine to every event he touches.

As the Wandering Chef, Jon has traveled, tasted, and trained across the world — bringing those flavors home to your table.
